Providing Quick And Personalised Professional Home-based Services

Ever since the pandemic started, as health facilities are constrained due to the overwhelming numbers and the risk of contracting contagious diseases, the Ministry of Health(MOH) has provided an option that Home-Based care is the safest method to care for all patients and all vulnerables(Above 60years). In periods where safety is key and our communities lack Affordable High Quality Home-Based Care, we come as an experirnced team to render the due service. It is on this premise that our team & expertise has consistently grown and assisted hundreds of seniors & home-based patients over the years to access quality health care in with respect and dignity. To this day, our team focuses on promoting the health and well-being of patients and families by providing expert, cost-effective care in their homes and communities.









Our Mission

To provide Timely, Professional Bed-side nursing care that is acceptable to all our clients and in a flexible manner.
